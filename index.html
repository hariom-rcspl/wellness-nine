Our Locations

As of June 2023, India was ranked tenth in the world on the Medical Tourism Index (MTI) with a score of 69.80. India is a top medical tourism destination and is considered one of the most cost-efficient and fastest-growing destinations in the world.

Here are some other details about medical tourism in India:

Medical services

India offers over 200 medical services, including fertility treatments, skincare, cardiovascular surgeries, cancer treatment, and organ transplant surgeries.

Medical tourism growth

The number of medical tourists in India has increased dramatically, with over a tenfold increase between 2014 and 2019. In 2024, Crisil estimated that 7.3 million medical tourists would visit India, up from 6.1 million in 2023.

Medical tourism hubs

South Indian states, including Tamilnadu, Andhra Pradesh, Kerala, Karnataka, and Telangana, are leading medical tourism hubs in India.
